WHat is ukraine visa?
Ukraine visa is an official document that stamps to your passport or travel document and permits the owner to cross the border of Ukraine and stay in Ukraine for a specified time period. The visa contains information about entry and leave date, visa type and the allowed number of entries, as well as owner's passport data and the information about the host organization (visa sponsor). Please note that visa is the document that permits both entry and exit; if you stay in Ukraine for a period that is longer than specified by the visa or lose the passport with the visa, leaving the country will be problematic
WHY DO I NEED AN INVITATION?
An invitation is one of the required documents for every visa application submitted to the Ukraine Consulate. It is required for every kind of visa and confirms purpose of you trip.
WHERE CAN I obtain A UKRAINE VISA?
To obtain a visa applicant should apply required documents to Consulate of Ukraine in his home country. If applicant don't have Consulate of Ukraine in his home country applicant should apply to Consulate of Ukraine at nearby country.
