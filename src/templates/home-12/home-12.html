HOW TO RECOGNIZE AND AVOID SCAMMERS?

  • If some one is telling you that you will get visa with guaranty - then you are almost become a fraud victim. Because no one can give you 100% guaranty for visa issuing. Since 2015 we've seen dozents of people who have became a fraud victim and lost even not hundreds bu thousands of dollars. So please be smart and careful.

  • Ask to show company documents. There should be company ID. You can check company profile using Ukrainian State Register website by entering this company ID. Some scammers could trying to cheat you by sending another company documents or fake documents. That's why important to check this company using State Register. And not less important to check is it really this person work at this company.

  • Never trust to reviews and testimonials on the company website, because most of all companies write fake reviews by themselves. Try to find company at Google Maps or Facebook and read reviews there because Google and Facebook do not allow to moderate and delete bad reviews. If company does not have Google or Facebook testimonials - it's very suspicious. Also scammers or indecent companies usually trying to hide bad reviews in Facebook. So you will not be able to see themWe sincerely do not recommend to deal with such companies.

  • Ask office and team photos. Or ask for video call from the company office. Then you will be able check how much real this company are. Try to seek some company style that can confirm that this photos or video really belong to this company because scammers like to use other companies photos or trying to impersonate an employee of another company, which they are really not. To avoid such things try to find more details about this company in Google. And try to call to company phones to check them.

  • Always try to think critically and check everything that you hear from someone who is trying to sell you something. Ask person to whom you are dealing with to send you some proofs that confirm his or his company reliability. For example:
    - website
    - landline company phone number (good companies have authomatic company styled greetings like every call-centre have)
    - office photos
    - team photos
    - happy clients photos
    - company documents (with indicated company ID). Necessarily check company ID using Official Ukrainian State Register website.
    - ask for signing an official agreement using company letterhead attested with company stamp

  • Usually scammers ask 100% payment in advance. In this case you are not protected from fraud. Better to find companies who offer step-by-step payment procedure.

    Also try to find companies who can propose online payment procedure using your Visa or MasterCard. Because every company who have such feature was checked and authorized by bank security service. And only after successful inspection bank allows to use online payment system.

    If somebody offers you only cash money transfer like WesternUnion or MoneyGram without offering any other option - we do not recommend to deal with such compamies or persons.
